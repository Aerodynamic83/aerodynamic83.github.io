About Skool

Skool is an all-in-one community platform launched in 2019 by Sam Ovens and Daniel Kang. It unites courses, discussions, events, and gamification into one distraction-free workspace.

Hosted on enterprise-grade AWS servers across the US & EU, Skool powers over 1,200 creator communities, offering built-in email drip campaigns, timezone-aware calendars, analytics dashboards, and an automatic referral program.

Every group receives a 14-day free trial and then a flat $99/month fee with unlimited members, courses, and 0% transaction fees on creator payouts. Creators report up to a 300% boost in engagement and 50% faster course completion thanks to gamified leaderboards and accountability circles.
